For pullquotes, section leads, and byline italics — where the voice softens.
Body Inter — Google Fonts · sans-serif
Inter is the working horse of the brand. Clean, readable at every size, and pairs with Playfair without competing. All body copy, navigation, buttons, and small-text UI lives in Inter. Weights: 300 (light), 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold).

08 · Clear Space & Minimum Size

Give it room.

The mark always needs breathing room. Minimum size rules protect legibility.
X
X
X
X
Clear space · X = ⅓ mark height, on all sides
Minimum size · digital

16 px wide. Below this, use the favicon variant only (thicker strokes). The logomark is not designed for sizes under 16px.

Minimum size · print

0.4 inches (10mm) wide. Below this, the sprig details will print muddy. Use the monogram instead at smaller sizes.

09 · Usage Rules

Do & don't.

The small things that protect the brand over years. Share this with any designer, VA, or printer who handles the marks.

Do

  • Use the logomark on cream, white, or photography
  • Maintain clear space of at least ⅓ mark height on all sides
  • Use the horizontal lockup where space is wider than tall
  • Pair marks only with Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or Inter
  • Use the SVG files whenever possible (scales perfectly)
  • Use the monogram when the full mark is too detailed for the surface
  • Reserve gold for foil / premium tier moments only

Don't

  • Place on busy photography without adjusting the photo's opacity
  • Stretch, skew, or rotate any mark
  • Change the colors outside the approved palette
  • Re-draw the sprig or modify the SVG geometry
  • Set the wordmark in fonts other than Playfair Display
  • Add drop shadows, glows, or filters
  • Use the mark on brightly colored backgrounds outside the palette
  • Use gold as a primary color (it's ornamental only)
