The quiet truth

You've done everything right.

You've counted. You've cut. You've logged. You've fasted. You've meal-prepped until your Sundays disappeared. You've bookmarked the podcasts, downloaded the apps, and bought the "lifestyle, not a diet" program that was, in fact, a diet.

And still — the voice in your head won't stop negotiating with every meal.

You tell your kids "all foods are okay." You know, in your bones, that you don't want them to inherit what you've been carrying. But privately, you're exhausted. Because you don't believe it about yourself yet.

If this sounds familiar, you are not alone. And — more importantly — you are not the problem.

The problem is that you were handed a system that profits every time you fail. And the harder you try to make it work, the tighter it grips.

There is another way. It doesn't look like a diet. It doesn't feel like deprivation. It doesn't run on willpower. It runs on awareness. And it lasts.

Is this for you?

A plain-spoken checklist.

This course is for you if…

  • You've tried "every diet" and they've all eventually failed you
  • You're tired of the mental load of tracking, measuring, earning, punishing
  • You want your kids to grow up with a different relationship to food than you did
  • You suspect the problem isn't your willpower — and you're ready to prove it to yourself
  • You want an approach that lasts longer than the next 90 days

This course is not for you if…

  • You're looking for a meal plan, macros, or a weight-loss program (this is neither)
  • You want rules to follow — the method is built on awareness, not prescription
  • You're currently in active treatment for an eating disorder. Please work with a qualified clinical team first. This is educational, not clinical care.

Questions

Before you decide.

Is this a weight loss program?
No. Weight change may or may not happen — for some students it does, for some it doesn't. This course is about your relationship with food, not the number on the scale. If you are looking strictly for weight loss, this is not the right course for you.
How long do I have access?
Forever. Including every future update. Buy once, keep for life.
How much time does it take each week?
About 45 minutes per week of video, plus 15–30 minutes of light workbook time. Less than one TV episode.
What if I fall behind?
You can't fall behind. The course is self-paced. Modules drip-release weekly, but once unlocked, they're yours to move through at your own speed.
Can I do this if I'm not a mom?
Yes. Roughly 30% of students do not have children at home. The core method is the same for everyone. Module 4 (The Family Table) is optional context for anyone not currently navigating family meals.
What's your refund policy?
A full 30-day peace-of-mind guarantee. If you complete the first two modules and don't feel the course is for you, email us within 30 days of purchase and we'll refund you in full — no questions, no forms, no friction.
Will I have access to Phyllis directly?
In the Core Course: through email support and monthly Q&A recaps. In the Family Edition: you'll have three months of live monthly group Q&A calls and private community access.
Is there a payment plan?
Yes. Both tiers are available as a single payment or as 3 monthly payments at a small processing uplift. Select at checkout.
